CypherCop is an online course that offers learning about ethical hacking in web environments. In this course, you will have access to intrusion techniques and system security used by major companies such as Google, Facebook, and the financial market. The course opens up new possibilities for you, with new paths, new ways of thinking, and seeing technology. With CypherCop, you can become an ethical hacker and gain valuable knowledge for the job market.
